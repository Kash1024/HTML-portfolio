Kash's Top Three Movies

My top 3 movies of all time are:

John Wick

John Wick a movie with 4 sequals that expresses the life of an assassin who after losing his wife, seems to encounter the worst and is forced to get even. He then wants to leave the assassin committee to just be done with all the killing.

Salt

This movie stars my favorite actress Angelina Jolie who plays as a CIA agent who is actaully undercover for a Russian spy. She finds love in America, but once the Russian doomsday comes and they look for her, they kill her lover which then forces her to find Her Russian family and kill them.

Lucy

Lucy is one of my most favorites because it gives an idea of what humans could be capable of if we were able to use 100% of our brain.
